Creative Painting and Stenciling is owned and operated by Jeanette Mosier from Wadsworth, Ohio. Jeanette has been painting and stenciling homes in Northeast Ohio and beyond since 1995. Her work has been featured in numerous nationwide publications such as Country Homes magazine. She specializes in a wide array of design techniques and only uses the highest quality materials available, so the job is always completed to the highest of standards and in a timely manner. There is no job too big or small for Jeanette. Creative Painting and Stenciling by Jeanette offers professional services and quality without the high professional cost.
